The Future of Hair & Beauty: How Rent-a-Chair is Changing the Game

12 April 2026 · 6 min read

How a new generation of stylists are taking ownership of their work - and what that means for the salons of tomorrow.

Ever wondered how your fave hairstylist manages their business behind the scenes? The beauty world is evolving fast, and there’s a trend called "rent-a-chair" that’s totally transforming salons.

But what does this mean for your go-to hair stylist or lash tech? And how can you help support them?

So, what is the Rent-a-Chair model?

In the past, beauty pros would just work as employees at salons. But now? Many are embracing the freedom of being their own boss! The rent-a-chair model lets freelancers work from established salons by renting their own space, giving them independence while still providing amazing services in a professional setting.

It’s basically a win for everyone:

  • Freelancers get to control their schedules, prices, and personal brand while working in a fully equipped salon.
  • Salons can make the most of their space and boost their income by renting out chairs.
  • Clients (that’s you!) still get expert services from professionals you trust in gorgeous salon environments.

Why more beauty pros are going freelance…

With freelancing becoming huge across all industries, beauty professionals are jumping on the trend too. Many talented stylists and beauty therapists are loving the flexibility of setting their own hours, building their personal brand, and connecting directly with clients.

The tricky part? Finding the perfect salon space.

How tech is making Rent-a-Chair easier

Thankfully, new platforms are making it super easy for salons and freelancers to connect. Companies like MuseAvenue are streamlining the whole process by letting salon owners list available chairs while giving freelancers a simple way to find professional spaces.

This means:

  • For Salons: They can maximise their space by renting chairs to independent beauty pros.
  • For Freelancers: They can find the perfect salon vibes without the massive costs of opening a full salon.
  • For You: If you love your stylist but they’re looking for a new workspace, knowing about these platforms can help them stay independent while still giving you amazing results.

How you can support the Rent-a-Chair movement

As clients, we have major influence on the future of beauty! Here’s how you can help:

  • Spread the word - If your stylist is thinking about going freelance, tell them about rent-a-chair opportunities. Platforms like MuseAvenue make finding the perfect salon space super simple.
  • Support independent beauty pros - Find your favourite freelancers on Bella, book directly with them, and tell your besties about their amazing work.
  • Help salon owners - Many salons are struggling with staffing, and rent-a-chair is a great solution. Mention MuseAvenue to your local salon - it could help them stay thriving in this changing industry.
  • Choose supportive salons - Next time you book that balayage or facial, ask if the salon offers rent-a-chair spaces for freelancers. This helps keep talented professionals in business and ensures you continue receiving top-tier beauty services.

The future of beauty is flexible

The rent-a-chair model is definitely here to stay, giving beauty professionals the freedom they want with the professional environment they need. With new tech making connections easier than ever, more beauty experts are choosing to go independent.

Next time you’re chatting with your stylist during your appointment, why not bring it up? Ask them about rent-a-chair and see what they think about the future of their career. You might just be the one who helps them discover an amazing new opportunity!
